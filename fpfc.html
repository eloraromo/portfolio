"Fluo Pink Fan Club," a collaborative project with Amanda Houttekier, Megan Meyer, and Anya McKee. 3 designs screen printed and block printed onto a collection of totes and thrifted clothing to represent a shared love for the risograph. The designs depict the risograph machine itself and the ink drums that go into the riso; the name “Fluo Pink Fan Club” is in reference to the bright fluorescent pink ink popular to riso.
