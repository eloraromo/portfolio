Elora Romo (she/her) is a 25 year-old multidisciplinary graphic designer and printmaker based in Richmond, VA. She graduated from Virginia Commonwealth University School of the Arts (VCUarts) in 2022 with a BFA in Graphic Design and a minor in Painting + Printmaking. Elora currently works as a full-time graphic designer for Performance Media Marketing, but is open to freelance or contract work on a case-by-case basis. Resume available at request.
